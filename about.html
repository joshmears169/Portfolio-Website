Hi! Let me tell you a little about myself..

Well, this year has been a crazy one. I have gone from working in Market Research in Winchester to travelling around South East Asia for 2 months and now I am back home in England enjoying the 'new normal'.

I quit my job before I went travelling with a view of finding another job when I got back. Then Corona Virus hit.

With a lack of opportunity, I decided that I wasn't going to let this get me down and I was going to focus on upskilling myself and having something to be proud of by the end of this virus.

I started learning Spanish, running 5k's, drawing and lastly, learning all about cloud computing (and a little web development!).

I am now very excited to say that I have three AWS certificates and have learnt the basics of python, sql and the command line. I am now creating projects to try to demonstrate my new skills, with a view of getting my first role in the cloud very soon.

This website is a product of my first project using the AWS Cloud. You can read all about the project and my experience here!
